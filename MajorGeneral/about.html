About Us

Instead of being just another gym, our founders wanted to be the best in what we do! Over the last decade Major Fitness has grown into one New York’s most popular gyms- helping our clients live longer, happier and healthier lives. For 10 years helping clients reach their fitness goals has been at the heart of what we do and why we do it! We live and breathe our core values.

We believe open, direct and supportive communication is key to success. We pride ourselves in being a safe and open space for our clients.We’re committed to common goals making our clients achieve greater results.
